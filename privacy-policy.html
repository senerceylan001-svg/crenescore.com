This Privacy Policy explains how CuppoTap ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile game CuppoTap (the "Game"), available on Google Play and the Apple App Store. By downloading or playing the Game, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). The Game does not require account registration or login.

However, the Game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain data to serve and measure ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view duration)
  • App usage data for ad performance measurement

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. On iOS devices, AdMob will request App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission before collecting the IDFA for personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

We use AdMob solely to display ads within the Game (banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads). We do not use any collected data for profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose beyond operating and improving the Game.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game integrates the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – advertising network by Google LLC.
    Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
    Data Processing Terms: business.safety.google/adsprocessorterms

We do not integrate any analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or social media platforms.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5 and later, the Game may display Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt asking for permission to track your activity across apps and websites. If you deny permission, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized based on your cross-app activity.

5. Children's Privacy

CuppoTap is rated for users aged 13 and older (Google Play) and 4+ (App Store, no objectionable content). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the Game, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Retention

We do not store any personal data on our own servers. Data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's own retention and deletion policies. You may request deletion of data associated with your Google account via Google Account settings.

7. Your Rights (GDPR / KVKK)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or Turkey, you may have rights regarding your personal data under GDPR or KVKK, including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to request correction or deletion
  • Right to object to or restrict processing
  • Right to data portability

For data processed by AdMob, please use Google's privacy controls. For other requests, contact us at the address below.

8. Opt-Out of Personalized Ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for CuppoTap

9. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect the Game. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy
