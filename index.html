CuppoTap
Casual Reflex Android · iOS

A color-matching reflex game where speed and precision decide your fate. Tap the right button, fire the right color, destroy falling balls — but one wrong move costs you dearly.

How it works

🎯
Shoot to match
Tap a color button at the bottom to fire that color from the shooter. Hit the matching falling ball to score.
↔️
Move the shooter
Press and drag to slide the shooter left or right. Positioning is everything.
🔀
Buttons shuffle
After score 40, the three color buttons swap positions every 30 seconds. Stay sharp.
💀
Two ways to lose
Score drops below 0, or a falling ball reaches your shooter. Don't let either happen.

Scoring

ActionEffect
Hit matching color ball+1 point
Hit wrong color ball−1 point
Hit red ball 🔴−5 points
Hit blue ball 🔵Shooter freezes temporarily

Ball colors

Green – match button
Purple – match button
Orange – match button
Blue – freezes shooter
Red – −5 points

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2025 · Applies to: CuppoTap (Android & iOS)

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Crenes Core ("we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile products, including the game CuppoTap, coming soon to Google Play and the Apple App Store. By using our products, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. Our apps do not require account registration or login.

However, our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region level)
  • Device type, OS version, and language settings
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view duration)

This data is processed by Google LLC under their Privacy Policy.

2. Third-party services

Our apps integrate Google AdMob only. We do not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting tools, or social media integrations.

3. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS 14.5+, our app may show Apple's ATT prompt requesting permission to track activity across apps. If you deny, you will still see ads — just not personalized ones.

4. Children's privacy

Our apps are rated for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has shared personal data through our app, contact us immediately.

5. Your rights (GDPR / KVKK)

If you are in the EU or Turkey, you may have rights including access, correction, deletion, and objection to processing. For AdMob data, use Google's privacy controls. For other requests, contact us below.

6. Opt-out of personalized ads

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for our app

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of our products constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
